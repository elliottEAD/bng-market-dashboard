Methodology and limitations

Removed records are excluded from the dashboard where they are marked as removed in the source dataset.

Registered supply is calculated from proposed habitat, hedgerow and watercourse extents recorded against registered gain sites. Allocated area and length are calculated from allocation component rows. Remaining figures represent remaining registered area or length, not remaining biodiversity units.

Allocation unit values are recorded at allocation level and may relate to multiple habitat components. Unit totals are therefore deduplicated at allocation level to avoid counting the same allocation value multiple times across component habitat rows.

The public register does not provide sufficient metric data to calculate remaining biodiversity units with confidence. Indicative values are for market intelligence purposes only and are not confirmed transaction values.
